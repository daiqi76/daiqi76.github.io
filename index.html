About Me

Daiqi Liu 刘戴齐

Hello! I'm Daiqi Liu, a PhD student at the Pattern Recognition Lab (LME) at Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU), supervised by Prof. Andreas Maier and Dr. Paula Andrea Pérez-Toro.

My research focuses on AI-driven methods for articulatory movement analysis — including articulator segmentation, multimodal learning, and dynamic modeling of the vocal tract using real-time MRI. I work in close collaboration with Nyvenn de Castro and Prof. Jana Hutter from the Smart Imaging Lab.

Research Interests

  • Articulatory Analysis: Segmentation and dynamic modeling of vocal tract structures in real-time MRI
  • Multimodal Learning: Fusion of audio, video, and phonological features for speech analysis
  • Medical Image Processing: Deep learning for segmentation and analysis in clinical imaging
  • Speech-Language Pathology: AI tools to support diagnosis and treatment monitoring
